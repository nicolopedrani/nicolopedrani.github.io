About

My passion lies in exploring new technologies, specifically in the realm of artificial intelligence and machine learning. My objective is to enhance my knowledge and expertise in these areas to effectively address the upcoming challenges that the world is set to encounter. As such, I am seeking a work environment that fosters my curiosity and provides continuous opportunities for growth.

Currently I am a research engineer at Teoresi Spa, working as a consultant for Leonardo Spa, in the context of Aerospace & Defence. Specialized in crafting realistic simulations and implementing advanced algorithms using MATLAB and Python. My work involves modeling complex scenarios and analyzing data to drive innovation and enhance decision-making in the aerospace and defence industry.

I also worked as a data scientist at Deloitte Consulting. Specialized in the design of data science and advanced analytics tools to support the digital transformation of modern organizations through the implementation of data-driven mind-set and strategic framework. I have been working primarily on the developments of advanced analytics instruments for the energy industry.

I spent one year as a Ph.D. student at the Atomistic Simulations group at the Italian Institute of Technology, (Genova, Italy) under the supervision of Prof. Michele Parrinello. My research activity was mostly focused on the integration of machine learning and enhanced sampling techniques to study rare events in physics, chemistry and biology. Examples are the data-driven identification of collective variables. I am specialized in analyzing data coming from complex system dynamics, with the aim to detect hidden structures and relationships and their related errors and fluctuations.

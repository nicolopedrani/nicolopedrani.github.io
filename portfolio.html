  • Google Project Management Portfolio Project

    Sauce & Spoon Tablet Rollout

    The aim of this project was to pilot the rollout of tabletop tablet menus at two of Sauce & Spoon's five locations. This initiative sought to enhance service speed, optimize table turnover, and increase guest capacity. Furthermore, the implementation of these tablets provided a new avenue for data collection to help ensure the restaurant's ongoing success

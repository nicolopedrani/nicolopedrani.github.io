About

Welcome! My name is Nicolò, and I am deeply passionate about exploring new technologies, especially in artificial intelligence and machine learning. My goal is to continuously expand my knowledge and skills in these fields to tackle the emerging challenges of our world. I thrive in environments that nurture curiosity and provide continuous growth opportunities.

Currently, I serve as a research engineer at Teoresi Spa, where I work as a consultant for Leonardo Spa within the Aerospace & Defence sector. My expertise lies in crafting realistic simulations and implementing advanced algorithms using MATLAB and Python. I focus on modeling complex scenarios and analyzing data to drive innovation and improve decision-making in the aerospace and defence industry.

Before my current role, I worked as a data scientist at Deloitte Consulting. There, I specialized in designing data science and advanced analytics tools to support the digital transformation of modern organizations. I primarily developed advanced analytics instruments for the energy industry, fostering a data-driven mindset and strategic framework.

My academic journey includes a year as a Ph.D. student at the Atomistic Simulations group at the Italian Institute of Technology in Genova, Italy, under the mentorship of Prof. Michele Parrinello. My research focused on integrating machine learning and enhanced sampling techniques to study rare events in physics, chemistry, and biology. I specialized in analyzing complex system dynamics to uncover hidden structures and relationships, alongside understanding their errors and fluctuations.

Beyond my professional and academic endeavors, I have a rich tapestry of personal interests. I am a former professional soccer player, having played for AC COMO. This experience instilled in me the values of teamwork, discipline, and resilience. I also have a passion for traveling, as it allows me to experience new cultures and meet diverse people, enriching my life and broadening my perspective. Additionally, I am an avid reader, always seeking to learn new things and expand my knowledge across various domains.
